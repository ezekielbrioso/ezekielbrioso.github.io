Welcome to Ghibli Glimpse

Discover the magic of Studio Ghibli films

Nausicaä of the Valley of the Wind

Release Date: 1984

A princess fights to prevent the destruction of her world by a toxic jungle and the war between humans and nature.

Castle in the Sky

Release Date: 1986

A young boy and girl search for a legendary floating island, but are pursued by pirates and government agents determined to capture it.

Grave of the Fireflies

Release Date: 1988

A young boy and his little sister struggle to survive in Japan during World War II after their home is destroyed.

My Neighbor Totoro

Release Date: 1988

A heartwarming story of two girls who move to the countryside and discover a magical creature named Totoro.

Kiki's Delivery Service

Release Date: 1989

A young witch named Kiki starts her own delivery service, learning about independence, responsibility, and self-discovery along the way.

Only Yesterday

Release Date: 1991

A 27-year-old woman reflects on her childhood while traveling to the countryside, leading to a journey of self-discovery.

Porco Rosso

Release Date: 1992

Set in Italy during the 1930s, the film tells the story of a former World War I pilot who becomes a bounty hunter and is cursed with the appearance of a pig.

Ocean Waves

Release Date: 1993

A high school student reflects on his past relationships with two classmates during a school trip to Hawaii.

Pom Poko

Release Date: 1994

A group of shape-shifting raccoon dogs use their magical powers to protect their forest home from urban development.

Whisper of the Heart

Release Date: 1995

A young girl discovers her passion for writing and begins on a journey of self-discovery, guided by a mysterious antique cat figurine.

Princess Mononoke

Release Date: 1997

A young man begins on a journey to understand and heal the conflict between humans and the spirits of the forest.

My Neighbors the Yamadas

Release Date: 1999

A comedic look at the everyday lives of the Yamada family, highlighting the humor and challenges of family life.

Spirited Away

Release Date: 2001

A young girl, Chihiro, becomes trapped in a mysterious world of spirits and gods and must find a way to save her parents.

The Cat Returns

Release Date: 2002

A young girl is whisked away to the Cat Kingdom after saving a cat from an accident, only to be pursued by feline royalty who want her to marry their prince.

Howl's Moving Castle

Release Date: 2004

A young woman is cursed by a witch and transformed into an elderly woman, seeking refuge in a magical, moving castle owned by the mysterious wizard Howl.

Tales from Earthsea

Release Date: 2006

A young prince embarks on a journey to uncover the truth about his cursed bloodline while trying to stop a world-ending event.

Ponyo

Release Date: 2008

A young boy befriends a goldfish who turns into a girl, leading to a series of magical events that threaten to reshape the world.

The Secret World of Arrietty

Release Date: 2010

In a hidden world beneath the floorboards, tiny people known as Borrowers live secretly among humans. Arrietty befriends a boy and faces the dangers of being discovered.

From Up on Poppy Hill

Release Date: 2011

A young girl and a boy try to save their school clubhouse from being demolished while uncovering the history of their parents.

The Wind Rises

Release Date: 2013

A biographical film about the life of Jiro Horikoshi, the designer of the Mitsubishi A6M Zero fighter plane used in World War II.

The Tale of the Princess Kaguya

Release Date: 2013

A bamboo cutter finds a tiny girl inside a bamboo stalk and raises her as his daughter, unaware of her divine origins.

When Marnie Was There

Release Date: 2014

A lonely girl forms a bond with Marnie, a mysterious girl who may not be what she seems, in a haunting story of friendship and secrets.

Earwig and the Witch

Release Date: 2020

A headstrong orphan girl is adopted by a selfish witch and must use her cleverness to escape the witch's control and uncover secrets about her origins.

The Boy and the Heron

Release Date: 2023

A young boy begins on a surreal adventure following the death of his mother, guided by a mysterious heron that leads him through a fantastical journey filled with myth, magic, and self-discovery.
